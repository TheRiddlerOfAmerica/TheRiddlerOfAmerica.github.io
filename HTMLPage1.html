The Curious Case of a Town Called Oakmont

The Curious Case of a Town Called Oakmont is a DnD / Call of Cthulhu campaign. It was originally inspired by the game called The Sinking City. I have taken many aspects of their game world and have expanded it greatly. This campaign is a mystery, and great mystery campaigns are like ogres, which is to say ogres are like onions and have many layers.
